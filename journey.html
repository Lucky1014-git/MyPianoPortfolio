My Musical Journey

A timeline of growth and discovery

The Beginning

2019

Started piano lessons with basic scales and simple melodies. First encounter with the magic of creating music.

First Pieces

2020

Learned first classical pieces including "Für Elise" and simple Bach inventions. Discovered love for classical music.

First Performance

2021

Nervous but excited first public performance at school talent show. Realized the joy of sharing music with others.

Technical Development

2022

Focused on technique development with scales, arpeggios, and etudes. Started tackling more challenging repertoire.

Grade 8 & Recital

2023

Achieved Grade 8 certification and performed first solo recital. A major milestone in musical development.

Competition Success

2024

Won first place in regional competition. Recognition of years of dedication and continuous improvement.
